# Class 9 Mathematics — Topic List (SAMPLE, synthetic)

> Synthetic sample topic outline for the Teacher Copilot demo. Not copied from any
> textbook.

## Number systems
Rational and irrational numbers, representing real numbers on the number line, laws of
exponents for real numbers, and rationalising denominators. Students should be able to
locate numbers like the square root of two on a number line by construction.

## Polynomials
Definition of a polynomial in one variable, degree, zeroes of a polynomial, the
Remainder Theorem and the Factor Theorem, and factorisation of quadratic and cubic
polynomials using standard algebraic identities.

## Coordinate geometry
The Cartesian plane, plotting points given their coordinates, and reading coordinates of
points marked on a grid. Emphasis on the sign conventions across the four quadrants.

## Linear equations in two variables
Solutions of a linear equation as ordered pairs, drawing the graph of a linear equation
as a straight line, and interpreting the graph. Every point on the line is a solution
and every solution lies on the line.

## Triangles
Congruence of triangles and the criteria SSS, SAS, ASA, and RHS. Properties of
isosceles triangles and simple inequalities relating sides and angles of a triangle.

## Suggested pacing
Number systems and polynomials are typically taught first as they underpin later
algebra. Coordinate geometry pairs naturally with linear equations in two variables.
